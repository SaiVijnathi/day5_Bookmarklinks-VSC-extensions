Top IOS and Android phones

The best IOS phones

iOS is the operating system that runs on Apple's iPhone and iPad devices. It's designed to work with multi-touch devices and uses gestures like swiping, pinching, and tapping. iOS (formerly iPhone OS) is a mobile operating system developed by Apple exclusively for its smartphones. It was unveiled in January 2007 for the first-generation iPhone, launched in June 2007. It is the operating system that powers many of the company's mobile devices, including the iPhone. Information security. Information security companies unanimously agree that Apple devices are more secure than Android devices. According to a report by Forbes, 97 percent of smartphone malware targets Android phones.

The best Android phones

Android phones are smartphones that run on the Android operating system (OS) developed by Google. They are made by many manufacturers, including Samsung, OnePlus, Sony, and Nokia. Surprisingly, Android phones make up over 70% of all phones bought across the world, while Apple's iOS-powered iPhones only account for 27%. The UK and US has a much larger share of iPhones than most other countries, but Android phones are still roughly as popular over here too.
